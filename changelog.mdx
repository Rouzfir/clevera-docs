<Update label="10/20/2025">
  ## New documentation integrations

  You can now export and publish Clevera articles directly to popular documentation and support platforms, including Zendesk, Notion, Confluence, GitBook, HelpScout, GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, ClickUp, Google Docs, and ReadMe, making it easier to keep your knowledge base in sync.

<Update label="10/19/2025">
  ## Article generation and editor now available

  Clevera can now turn your screen recordings into detailed, structured articles alongside your videos. This release introduces a full article editor with AI assisted rewriting, automatic screenshots, and flexible formatting, making it easy to publish complete video plus article documentation from a single recording.
</Update>
