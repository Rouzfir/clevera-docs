<Update label="06/04/2026">
    ## Scene editor improvements
  We refreshed the scene editor, including transitions, title cards, logo reveals, and the insert menu, to make editing feel smoother and easier to navigate. These updates also improve light mode compatibility across the editor UI.

  ## Timeline and segment editing
  Video segments now appear as frames in the main timeline and load faster, so you can review and adjust them more quickly. We also improved playhead behavior, zoom controls, and revision handling so it’s easier to track and restore changes while you work.

  ## Voiceovers and audio
  You can now delete event voiceovers, and add voiceovers for both freeze and play segments. We also improved the sound effect selector and background music controls, making it simpler to choose and manage audio for scenes and transitions.

  ## Translation, compatibility, and rendering
  New scenes are translated more reliably, including card text, so localized recordings stay aligned as you edit. We also improved support for older recordings and tightened render behavior for transitions, title cards, logo reveals, and other scene animations to make exports more dependable.
</Update>

<Update label="10/19/2025">
  ## Article generation and editor now available

  Clevera can now turn your screen recordings into detailed, structured articles alongside your videos.
